Beef Bulgogi

Description

This recipe introduces you to the delightful world of Beef Bulgogi, a Korean dish that combines tender slices of beef with a tantalizing marinade. The beef is thinly sliced and bathed in a harmonious blend of soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and other flavorful ingredients. This marinade imparts a wonderful depth of flavor to the meat, infusing it with a perfect balance of savory and slightly sweet notes. When cooked, the beef becomes succulent and caramelized, boasting a mouthwatering combination of tenderness and charred edges. The result is a dish that is both hearty and satisfying, and when served with steamed rice and accompanied by crisp vegetables, it creates a truly delicious and memorable dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 2 1/2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lb flanked steak, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk soy sauce, green onion, sugar, garlic, sesame seeds, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl
  3. Place flank steak slices in a shallow dish. Pour marinade over top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate
  5. Quickly grill flank steak slices on the preheated grill until slightly charred and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes per side
  6. Serve hot and enjoy
