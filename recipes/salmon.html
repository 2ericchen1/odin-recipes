Salmon

Description

This recipe features succulent salmon fillets coated in a mouthwatering honey garlic glaze. The glaze perfectly balances the sweetness of honey with the savory notes of garlic, resulting in a delectable combination of flavors. The salmon is cooked to perfection, with a tender and flaky texture that effortlessly complements the rich glaze. This dish offers a harmonious blend of sweet and tangy elements, making it a delightful option for seafood enthusiasts seeking a delicious and memorable meal.

Ingredients

Salmon

  • 4 (6 oz each) salmon filets
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika (or regular paprika)
  • 1/4 tsp blackening seasoning (optional)
Sauce
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
Steps
  1. Pat salmon dry, then season with salt, pepper, paprika and blackening seasoning (if using)
  2. Adjust oven rack to middle position, then preheat broiler. If you broil this with the rack up high the sauce will burn
  3. Add butter and oil to a large, oven-safe skillet over MED-HIGH heat. Once butter is melted, add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey and lemon juice and cook 30 seconds or so, until sauce is heated through
  4. Add salmon, skin side down (if using salmon with skin), and cook 3 minutes. While salmon cooks, baste frequently with sauce from the pan by spooning it over the top of the salmon
  5. Broil salmon for 5-6 minutes, basting with sauce once during the broil, until salmon is caramelized and cooked to desired doneness
  6. Serve and enjoy
